This is my story.

Hi, my name is Patrick Lemmer and I am a web developer from Germany. I currently learn all about Frontend-development at CareerFoundry. My goal is to pivot into a new career in the digital world. While I am now learning all about web development for the first time, I have been teaching myself some HTML and CSS in the past when I needed to make some changes to my personal website about photography. Also I have started another online course about frontend development while I was working in my previous job. My goal back then was to gain some expertise in the field, in order to switch roles within the company. As part of a project management team, I used to work together with developers and I decided to switch sides to also make use of my project management background and incoorperate my skills with the development part.

The story behind the name

As of February 2022, I am running this page under the name Meerkat Studio as a tribute to my late dad. The meerkat was one of his favourite animals and him and I had a special connection to each other through the love for animals. Meerkat Studio will offer services within the digital space, such as Full-Stack web development, freelance photography and post-production of both stills and video.
